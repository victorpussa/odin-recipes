Hot Pepper Sauce - A Trinidadian Staple

A green pepper sauce. Delicious!

A quick and simple hot sauce to go along with your dishes.

Run from those randomly hot ones and try out this seasoned pepper sauce.

Ingredients

  • 15 habanero peppers
  • 1 small mango - peeled, seeded and cut into chunks
  • 1 onion, roughly chopped
  • 3 green onions, roughy chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, roughly chopped
  • 1½ cups distilled white vinegar
  • 2 limes, juiced
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 4 tablespoons dry mustard powder
  • 1 tablespoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon curry powder
  • ½ teaspoon grated lime zest

Directions

  1. Wearing disposable gloves, and being careful not to get any in your eyes or on your skin, roughly chop the habanero peppers.
  2. Place the habanero peppers, mango, onion, green onions, and garlic into a blender.
  3. Pour in the vinegar, lime juice, and vegetable oil, cover the blender, and pulse until the mixture is very finely chopped.
  4. Stop the blender, and add dry mustard powder, salt, curry powder, and lime zest. Blend again until the sauce is smooth.
  5. Pour into clean jars, and store in refrigerator.
